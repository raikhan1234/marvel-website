About Marvel

Marvel is a media franchise and shared universe centered on a series of superhero films and television series produced by Marvel Studios.

Marvel Characters

Some of the most beloved characters include Spider-Man, Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, and many more. Each character has a unique story and journey within the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

Marvel Comics

The Marvel comic book universe is vast, with thousands of issues published since the 1930s. Iconic series like "The Amazing Spider-Man" and "The Avengers" have captivated audiences for generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Marvel Universe is a fictional universe where the stories of Marvel Comics are set. It features numerous superheroes, villains, and complex storylines.

The Avengers are a team of superheroes in the Marvel Universe who come together to fight threats too large for any single hero to handle.

S.H.I.E.L.D. (Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division) is a fictional espionage and law-enforcement agency in the Marvel Universe.

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a billionaire inventor and industrialist who becomes a superhero after creating a powered exoskeleton suit.

Wakanda is a fictional African nation in the Marvel Universe. It is technologically advanced and home to the superhero Black Panther.

Thanos is a powerful villain in the Marvel Universe, known for his quest to collect all six Infinity Stones to achieve ultimate power.

The Infinity Stones are six powerful gems that each grant unique abilities. They include the Space, Mind, Reality, Power, Time, and Soul Stones.

The Multiverse in Marvel refers to the concept of multiple parallel universes where alternate versions of characters and events exist.

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a young superhero with spider-like abilities, known for his agility, web-slinging, and sense of responsibility.

Doctor Strange is a master of the mystic arts, protecting the world from magical and interdimensional threats as the Sorcerer Supreme.
